Frequently Asked Questions

What products do your beauticians use?
Our beauty professionals use salon-grade products from trusted brands that are suitable for various skin and hair types. If you have specific product preferences or allergies, please mention them during booking, and our beauticians will accommodate accordingly.
How do you ensure hygiene during services?
We maintain strict hygiene protocols. All our tools and equipment are sanitized before each service, and our beauticians follow proper sanitization practices. They use disposable items where necessary and fresh linens for each client.
Can I request a specific beautician for my service?
Yes, if you've been serviced by one of our beauticians before and would like to request them again, you can mention their name during booking. We'll try our best to accommodate your request based on the beautician's availability.
Do you offer services for special occasions like weddings?
Yes, we offer special bridal packages and group services for weddings and other special occasions. These can be customized based on your requirements. We recommend booking these services well in advance to ensure availability.
What should I prepare before the beautician arrives?
For most services, you'll just need a clean, well-lit space. For spa treatments, having a quiet area would be beneficial. Our beauticians bring all necessary equipment and products. Specific preparation instructions may be sent to you before the appointment, depending on the service booked.
