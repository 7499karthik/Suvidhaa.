Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to emergency electrical issues?
For emergency electrical services, we aim to have a professional at your location within 45-60 minutes of booking. Our emergency service is available 24/7, including weekends and holidays.
Are your electricians certified and insured?
Yes, all our electricians are licensed, certified, and insured. They undergo rigorous background checks and skill assessments before joining our platform to ensure the highest standards of service and safety.
What electrical safety precautions do your technicians take?
Our electricians follow strict safety protocols including proper equipment usage, power shutoff before work begins, thorough testing after completion, and adherence to all electrical codes and standards. Safety is our top priority for both our professionals and customers.
Do you provide electrical services for commercial spaces?
Yes, we offer electrical services for both residential and commercial properties. Our electricians are experienced in handling complex commercial electrical systems, industrial wiring, and business-specific electrical needs.
Can your electricians install smart home electrical systems?
Absolutely! Our electricians are trained in modern smart home electrical installations including smart switches, automated lighting systems, smart thermostats, and home automation integration. We can help upgrade your home with the latest electrical technology.
