Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical cleaning session take?
The duration depends on the size of your home and the cleaning package selected. A basic cleaning for a 1BHK apartment typically takes 2-3 hours, while deep cleaning for larger homes can take 5-8 hours. Our team will provide an estimated timeframe when you book.
What cleaning products do you use?
We use professional-grade cleaning products that are effective yet safe. We also offer eco-friendly and hypoallergenic options upon request. If you have specific products you prefer, our team can use those as well.
Do I need to provide any equipment or supplies?
No, our cleaning professionals bring all necessary equipment and supplies including vacuum cleaners, mops, microfiber cloths, and cleaning solutions. However, if you have specialized cleaning tools for specific items in your home, you can make them available.
Is it safe to leave my home during the cleaning?
Yes, all our cleaning professionals are background-checked, verified, and insured. You can be present or leave during the cleaning process. Many customers prefer to provide access instructions and return to a clean home.
Do you offer recurring cleaning services?
Absolutely! We offer weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly recurring cleaning services at discounted rates. Regular cleaning schedules help maintain a consistently clean home and you can save up to 20% compared to one-time bookings.
