Detailed Process

Everything you need to know about booking services through Suvidhaa

1. Finding the Right Service

Start by visiting our homepage or using the search function to find the service you need. You can browse by category or search directly for specific services.

  • Enter your location to find providers in your area
  • Filter results by price range, ratings, or availability
  • View detailed profiles of service providers including their qualifications, experience, and customer reviews

2. Booking and Scheduling

Once you've found your preferred service provider, booking an appointment is quick and easy.

  • Select your preferred date and time from the provider's available slots
  • Add any specific requirements or instructions for the service provider
  • Receive instant confirmation via email and SMS
  • Get reminders 24 hours before your scheduled appointment

3. Service Delivery

On the day of your appointment, your service provider will arrive at the scheduled time.

  • Receive notifications when the provider is on their way
  • Track their arrival in real-time through our app
  • Service providers come with proper identification and necessary tools
  • All work is performed according to the agreed-upon terms

4. Payment and Feedback

After the service is completed, you can complete the transaction securely through our platform.

  • Pay securely using credit/debit cards, digital wallets, or UPI
  • Receive digital receipts and invoices instantly
  • Rate your service provider and provide feedback on your experience
  • Your reviews help maintain quality and assist other users in making informed decisions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about using Suvidhaa

How do I know the service providers are trustworthy?

+

All service providers on our platform undergo a thorough verification process that includes identity verification, background checks, and skill assessment. Additionally, you can read reviews from other customers to make an informed decision.

What if I need to reschedule or cancel my appointment?

+

You can easily reschedule or cancel your appointment through our website or app up to 4 hours before the scheduled time without any penalty. For cancellations made less than 4 hours in advance, a small cancellation fee may apply.

Is there a guarantee for the services provided?

+

Yes, all services booked through Suvidhaa come with our satisfaction guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the quality of service, you can report it within 48 hours, and we'll arrange for the issue to be resolved or provide a refund.

How are service prices determined?

+

Service providers set their own prices based on their expertise, experience, and market rates. We ensure that all prices are transparent and communicated upfront before you book the service. There are no hidden charges.

What areas do you currently serve?

+

Suvidhaa is currently available in major cities across India including Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Pune, and their surrounding areas. We're rapidly expanding to new locations - check our app for the latest coverage areas.
