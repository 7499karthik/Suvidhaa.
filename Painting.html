Frequently Asked Questions

How long will it take to paint my home?
The time required depends on the size of your space and complexity of the job. Typically, a standard room takes 1-2 days, while a complete 2-bedroom apartment may take 3-5 days. Our team provides a detailed timeline during the initial assessment.
What type of paints do you use?
We use premium quality, eco-friendly paints from leading brands like Asian Paints, Berger, Dulux, and Nerolac. We offer various options including matte, semi-gloss, and gloss finishes, as well as specialized paints for different surfaces and requirements.
Do I need to move my furniture before painting?
While our team can move smaller furniture pieces, we recommend moving larger or valuable items before the service. We use professional-grade drop cloths and plastic coverings to protect your furniture and flooring during the painting process.
Can you help with color selection?
Yes, our professional painters provide free color consultation services. They can recommend suitable color schemes based on your space, lighting conditions, existing decor, and personal preferences. We can also provide sample patches to help you visualize the final result.
Do you offer texture painting and special effects?
Absolutely! Our painters are skilled in creating various textured finishes and special effects including stucco, sponging, color washing, metallic finishes, and accent walls. These premium services are quoted separately based on complexity and area coverage.
