Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to emergency plumbing issues?
For emergency plumbing services, we aim to have a professional at your location within 60-90 minutes of booking. Our emergency service is available 24/7, including weekends and holidays.
Do your plumbers carry spare parts with them?
Yes, our plumbers carry commonly required spare parts and tools. For specific parts or custom fixtures, they might need to source them separately, which they will discuss with you during the service visit.
What is covered under your service warranty?
Our service warranty covers any issues that arise due to the service provided. If the same problem recurs within 30 days of our service, we'll fix it for free. For premium services, we offer an extended warranty of up to 1 year on parts and labor.
How are your service providers vetted?
All our service providers undergo a rigorous verification process including background checks, skill assessment, and training. We verify their identity, professional certifications, and experience before they join our platform.
Can I reschedule a booked service?
Yes, you can reschedule your service through the app or website at least 4 hours before the scheduled time without any penalty. For emergency rescheduling, please contact our customer support.
