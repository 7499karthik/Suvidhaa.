Our Story

From a simple idea to a thriving community marketplace

Bridging the Gap

Suvidhaa was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to create a seamless connection between local customers and skilled service providers. What began as a solution to everyday challenges has grown into a thriving marketplace that strengthens communities.

Founded in 2023, we recognized the frustration people faced when searching for reliable local services. Whether it was finding a plumber for an emergency repair or connecting with a tutor for academic support, the process was often time-consuming and uncertain.

Our founder's personal experience with these challenges inspired the creation of Suvidhaa - a platform that makes accessing quality local services as simple as a few clicks.

Growing Together

Starting in one neighborhood, Suvidhaa quickly expanded as both customers and service providers recognized the value of our platform. What makes us unique is our commitment to quality, trust, and community.

Today, we proudly connect thousands of customers with skilled professionals across dozens of service categories. Our network of verified providers delivers exceptional service quality while building their businesses and contributing to local economic growth.

As we continue to grow, we remain focused on our core mission of simplifying access to local services while maintaining the highest standards of quality and trust.

Our Journey

Key milestones that mark our path of growth

January 2023

The Beginning

Suvidhaa was founded with a vision to transform local service connections. Our first office opened with a team of 5 dedicated members.

April 2023

Platform Launch

We launched our first version of the platform in Delhi NCR with 50 verified service providers across 10 categories.

August 2023

Reaching Milestones

Celebrated our 1,000th service booking and expanded our provider network to 200 professionals.

Expansion
