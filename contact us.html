Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about contacting and working with us

How quickly will I get a response to my inquiry?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend contacting our support team directly by phone.

I'm a service provider. How can I join Suvidhaa?

You can apply to become a provider by clicking the "Become a Provider" button at the top of the page or by contacting our provider relations team at providers@suvidhaa.com. We'll guide you through the verification and onboarding process.

How do I report an issue with a service I received?

If you've experienced any issues with a service, please contact our customer support team immediately. You can reach us through the contact form on this page, by email at support@suvidhaa.com, or by phone. Please provide details about your booking and the issue you encountered.

Do you have offices in cities other than Gurugram?

Currently, our headquarters is in Gurugram, but we have regional teams operating in Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi, Hyderabad, and Chennai. We're rapidly expanding to more cities across India.

I have a suggestion for improving your platform. How can I share it?

We love hearing ideas from our community! You can share your suggestions through our contact form by selecting "Feedback" as the subject, or email them directly to feedback@suvidhaa.com. Our product team reviews all suggestions.
