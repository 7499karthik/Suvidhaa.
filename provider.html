Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about becoming a service provider

How long does the application process take? +

The typical application process takes 2-3 business days. Once you submit your application, our team will review your credentials and experience. If approved, you'll receive an email with next steps to complete your profile.

What documents do I need to register? +

You'll need to provide a valid government ID (Aadhar Card, PAN Card, or Driving License), proof of address, and any relevant professional certifications for your service category.

How do I receive payments? +

Payments are processed securely through our platform. After a service is completed and confirmed by the customer, the payment will be transferred to your registered bank account within 2-3 business days.

What fees does Suvidhaa charge? +

Suvidhaa charges a modest service fee of 10-15% per transaction, depending on your service category. This fee covers platform maintenance, marketing efforts, and customer support services.

Can I set my own prices? +

Yes, you have full control over your service pricing. We provide guidelines based on market rates, but you can set your prices according to your experience, expertise, and the complexity of services you offer.
